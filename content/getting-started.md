## Overview

This portal is your home base. It walks you through onboarding step by step, gives you a daily task sheet to log your work, and is where you submit your end-of-week report. Work through it top to bottom — each phase unlocks the next.

> Edit this guide by editing `content/getting-started.md` in the repo. The page below renders straight from that file, so it always reflects the latest version.

## For VAs

### Your onboarding path

You move through four steps, in order. Each one unlocks the next:

1. **Phase 0 — Getting Started** (this page). Read it, then click **Mark complete** at the top or bottom.
2. **Phase 1 — System Access.** Confirm you have access to every tool. Each item unlocks the next once you mark it done.
3. **Phase 2 — Foundations.** Work through the training lessons in order.
4. **Phase 2.1 — Core SOPs.** Read and acknowledge the company SOPs.

A phase you haven't unlocked yet shows a 🔒 in the top nav and a locked screen if you open it — that's expected, not a bug. Finish the step before it and it opens.

### Your dashboard (Home)

Home shows your overall progress, your weekly hours, and a card for each phase. Locked phases are dimmed with a 🔒. Start at the top and work down.

### The task sheet

Your **Task Sheet** is where you log daily work. Each row is a task; each column is a day. Click a cell to log time (or use the built-in timer). Totals roll up automatically.

- **Custom tasks** — add your own recurring tasks with **+ Task**.
- **Roles** — add a **+ Role** for a standing responsibility (e.g. "Creative Engine"). A role is time-tracked like a task and can hold sub-tasks; expand it to log time per sub-task, or collapse it to log at the role level.
- **Export Sheet** and **Generate EOW Report** live at the top of the task sheet.

> Log your time every working day. Consistent logging is what the team uses to see you're active.

### End-of-week (Fridays)

On Fridays, complete your EOW tasks and use **Generate EOW Report** on the task sheet to produce your weekly summary.

### The assistant

The chat bubble in the bottom-right answers questions about SOPs, tools, and policies. Ask it anything — it draws only from company documents.

## For Admins

### Inviting VAs

Invite members from **Admin → Users**. Each invite creates their account and returns a one-time link to set their password.

### Setting up Phase 1

In **Phase 1**, use the **Founder Setup** tab (pick the member) to mark each access item done or N/A. The member completes their own **Your Checklist** tab afterward.

### Tracking progress & performance

**Admin** shows onboarding progress; **Performance** shows weekly activity and consistency across the team.

### Managing content

Edit lessons, SOPs, and task links under **Admin → Content**. Add learning resources on the **Resources** page.
